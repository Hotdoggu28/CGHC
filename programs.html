Programs Offered

College of Accountancy and Entrepreneurship

The College of Accountancy and Entrepreneurship offers two academic degree programs. College of Medical Technology

The College of Medical Technology, since 2012, provides the best learning experience through its competent faculty and updated teaching facilities.

College of Nursing

BS Nursing is a four-year degree program with a strong educational foundation in liberal arts, sciences, and professional nursing courses taught with a transdisciplinary approach.

BS Physical Therapy

A four-year program that prepares graduates to work as professionals who advance the best possible health and function for individuals at all stages of life when their ability to move and function is threatened by aging, injury, illness disorders, conditions, or environmental factors.

BS Psychology

This program requires students to have internship in clinical and industrial settings that provides training opportunities to equip students towards a career in academic teaching and research, clinical practice, community and organizational development, counseling, psychotherapeutic intervention, human resources management and psychometrics.

BS Radiologic Technology

Bachelor of Science in Radiologic Technology is a four-year degree program that affords training in general radiography and special fields of Radiology.

Doctor of Medicine

Doctor of Medicine is a duly-recognized 4-year post baccalaureate program of basic and advanced clinical courses in Western and Eastern Medicine fundamentals and multidisciplinary practices.

Senior High School

CGHC Senior High School offers the Academic track with strands in Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics (STEM) and Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM). These two years of specialized upper secondary education aims to provide students with necessary knowledge, skills and relevant experience for higher education.

Caregiving NC II

The course is designed to develop the knowledge, skills and attitude of Caregivers in accordance with industry standards. It consists of competencies that a person must achieve to provide care and support to infants/toddlers, children, elderly, with special needs, and other essential needs of patients.
